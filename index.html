About

The IBM Mayflower Project is a pioneering effort to create an autonomous, AI-driven research vessel. Powered by IBM’s cutting-edge technologies in artificial intelligence, automation, and edge computing, the ship is designed to navigate oceans without human intervention. Its mission: to collect environmental data and explore new ways of conducting sustainable, unmanned marine research — all while retracing the original Mayflower's transatlantic route.

History

Over 400 years ago, the original Mayflower set sail from Plymouth, England, carrying 102 Pilgrims on a historic journey to the New World. Their voyage across the Atlantic in 1620 marked a defining moment in history, symbolizing exploration, courage, and the pursuit of new beginnings. Today, the IBM Mayflower Autonomous Ship retraces that same transatlantic route — not with people, but with purpose. Driven by AI and powered by solar energy, it reflects the spirit of the original voyage while showcasing the future of ocean exploration.
